Question's & Answer's

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the homepage. Follow the instructions to register using your email or social media accounts. Once registered, you'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Is there a fee to use the job portal?

No, our job portal is completely free for job seekers. You can browse and apply for jobs without any charges. Employers may have different subscription plans and options based on their hiring needs.

How can I search for jobs?

Once logged in, use the search bar on the homepage to filter jobs by title, location, industry, and more. You can also set up job alerts to receive notifications about new listings that match your criteria. Our advanced search options help you find the perfect job quickly.

How do I apply for a job?

After finding a job that interests you, click on the job listing to view detailed information about the position. Then, click the "Apply" button to submit your application, which will usually require uploading your resume and cover letter. You can track your application status in your profile.

Can I save jobs to apply later?

Yes, you can save jobs by clicking the "Save Job" button on the job listing page. These saved jobs will be stored in the "My Saved Jobs" section of your profile. You can revisit and apply for them whenever you're ready.

How can I contact customer support?

If you need assistance, visit the "Contact Us" page on our website. You can fill out the contact form or use the provided email address or phone number to reach our support team. We're available to help with any questions or issues you may have.
